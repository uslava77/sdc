About Street Dance Calendar

Street Dance Calendar (SDC) is a community platform dedicated to promoting and connecting the vibrant street dance scene across North America. Our mission is to create a centralized hub where dancers, event organizers, and enthusiasts can discover upcoming events, battles, workshops, and performances.

Our Mission

We aim to strengthen the street dance community by improving access to information about events, facilitating connections between dancers, and supporting event organizers with increased visibility for their gatherings.

Submit Your Event

Are you organizing a street dance event in North America? We'd love to feature it on our platform! Simply fill out our event submission form with all the details, and we'll review your submission.
